Protection Modules
Six layers of defense.
All enabled by default.

Every module works together to give you the most comprehensive browser protection available. No configuration needed.

🛡️

Ad Blocking

500+ built-in rules plus 15 community filter lists including EasyList, EasyPrivacy, HaGeZi DNS, and Peter Lowe's. Over 100,000 rules with auto-updates. Zero "acceptable ads" — no paid whitelists.

🔍

Smart Tracker Learning

Automatically detects which third-party domains track you across 3+ websites and blocks them. No predefined list needed — behavioral detection like Privacy Badger, but alongside filter lists for maximum coverage.

🔒

Fingerprint Protection

Blocks canvas fingerprinting, WebGL renderer spoofing, AudioContext noise injection, Battery API blocking, navigator property normalization. Sends GPC and DNT signals automatically.

🍪

Cookie Auto-Reject

Automatically clicks "Reject All" on cookie consent popups. Supports OneTrust, Cookiebot, Didomi, TrustArc, Quantcast, Klaro, and 20+ other CMP frameworks. Multi-language support.

🧹

Annoyance Blocker

Kills newsletter popups, chat widgets (Intercom, Drift, Tidio, Tawk), push notification prompts, app install banners, sticky video players, and exit-intent overlays.

⛏️

Crypto Miner Blocker

Stops cryptocurrency mining scripts from hijacking your CPU. Blocks CoinHive, CryptoLoot, JSECoin, CoinImp, and 15+ other known mining services and inline mining scripts.
